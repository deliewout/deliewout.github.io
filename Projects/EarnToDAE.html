Earn To DAE 3D

The game made for a gamejam with 7 other programmers made in the s&box engine. It's similar to the "earn to die games", where you have to drive though the city and drive over zombies in your cool upgradeable car.

Project Details

The game was made in the s&box engine, which was still in early access, and uses the C# coding language.

What I Learned

learning the s&box in 2.5 days was fun. It was interesting to see how the engine works and how it differs from other engines.
