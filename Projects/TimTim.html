TimTim

TimTim is the first game I developed with a group existing of 5 members, 3 programmers and 2 artists. This project was entirely developed in Unreal C++, except for linking up the RFX and SFX. Those were implemented by linking making events and broadcasting them for when they get called.

Project Details

This project was mostly developed while working an a team in our time at home with a weekly meetup in the classroom and a feedback moment from our teachers, which meant development needed to happen on top of other class projects. We used Unreal C++ for the game development, HacknPlan for organizing tasks and tracking progress, and Perforce for version control to manage our codebase effectively.
