Fire Escape Dispatch

Fire Escape Dispatch is a game I developed as part of a group project for a client. Working in a team, we utilized Unreal Engine with C++ for game development, HacknPlan for project management and task tracking, and Perforce for version control to ensure smooth collaboration. Throughout the project, we actively incorporated feedback from both our client and teacher, iterating on the design and implementation to meet requirements and improve the final product.
