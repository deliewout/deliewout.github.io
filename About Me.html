About Me

Who am I

My name is Wout Delie and I am Belgian student currently studying Game Development at Digital Arts and Entertainment in Howest Kortrijk. My interests are mostly in gameplay programming, focused on proceduralism and AI.

Why Game Development?

Studies

In high school I started in Moderne-clil, which means I had some courses in English taught instead of Dutch. 2 years later I had to make a choice in what I would pursue next and I decided to learn more about science, which was something that peaked my interest at the time. Again after 2 years I had to make another decision, this time i choice to follow human sciences. I was not sure if this was gonna be the right choice but in the end this was the study I finished my high school career with. Then I had to make the biggest decision, what did I want to study in college. Then I saw it "Game Development" and I was sold. I had gotten an interest in Game Development in my final year of high school when I had to make a decision to do a group research project and I had decided to make a game about "de hertogen van Gruuthuse". But then covid hit and the entire group project was cancelled. This did not stop for me to pursue the idea of making games and thus I decided to start my studies at DAE.

Extracurricalar activities
