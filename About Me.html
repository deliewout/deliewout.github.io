About Me

My name is Wout Delie and I am Belgian student currently studying Game Development at Digital Arts and Entertainment in Howest Kortrijk. My interests are mostly in gameplay programming, focused on proceduralism and AI.

Studies

In high school I started in Moderne-clil, which means I had some courses taught in English instead of Dutch. Two years later I chose to pursue science, something that had peaked my interest at the time. After another two years I switched to human sciences — not my most certain choice, but the one I finished my high school career with.


Then came the biggest decision: what to study in college. That's when I saw it — Game Development — and I was sold. My interest had started in my final year of high school when my group chose to build a game about "de hertogen van Gruuthuse" for a research project. Covid cancelled it, but not my ambition. I enrolled at DAE and never looked back.

Extracurricular Activities

Since the start of my bachelor's I joined Howest's basketball group, and after two years became one of its hosts. During the 2025–2026 academic year I joined the presidium of Centaura as barkeeper, balancing managing the club café and organizing Monday night events — all while keeping full attendance in my classes.
